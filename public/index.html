💡 How It Works

⏱️

Dynamic Timezones

The calendar utilizes UTC timestamps. Your iOS/Android device automatically converts these to your mobile's current local timezone.

🔔

Live Match Alerts

Matches contain built-in notifications: Kickoff (0m), Halftime Score (45m), and Fulltime Final Score (1h45m). Scores sync dynamically!

🌳

Automatic Knockouts

As group matches finish, qualified teams automatically populate and update the placeholder names in the Round of 16 and subsequent rounds.
